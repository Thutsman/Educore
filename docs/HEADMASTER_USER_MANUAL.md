# Educore — Headmaster User Manual

A short guide to help you navigate the system and carry out everyday tasks.

---

## Logging in and the dashboard

After you log in, you land on **Dashboard**. Here you see a summary of the school: total students, fees, attendance, and class performance. Use the sidebar on the left to open any section.

---

## Sidebar menu

The main menu is on the left:

- **Dashboard** — School overview
- **Academic** — Students, Academics (classes, subjects, exams, grades), Scheme Book, Lesson Plans, Assignments, Assessments, Resources, Attendance
- **Finance** — Fees and expenses
- **Communication** — Messages, Parent Messages
- **Reports** — Term Reports, Class Analytics
- **Administration** — Staff, Assets, Analytics, User Management

Click an item to open that section.

---

## Creating and managing classes

1. Go to **Academic** → **Academics**.
2. Open the **Classes** tab.
3. Click **Add Class**.
4. Fill in:
   - **Class name** (e.g. Form 1A)
   - **Academic year**
   - **Level** (e.g. 1 for Form 1)
   - **Stream** (optional, e.g. A, B)
   - **Class teacher** (optional)
5. Click **Save**.

To change or remove a class, use the **Edit** (pencil) or **Delete** (trash) buttons on the row.

---

## Adding subjects

1. Go to **Academic** → **Academics**.
2. Open the **Subjects** tab.
3. Click **Add Subject**.
4. Enter **Subject name** (e.g. Mathematics), **Code** (e.g. MATH), and optional **Description**.
5. Click **Save**.

You can edit or delete subjects from the same list.

---

## Academics — other tabs

- **Exams** — Set up and manage exams (class, subject, date, type).
- **Grade Entry** — Enter or view marks for exams.

---

## Students

Go to **Academic** → **Students** to see the student list. You can search, filter by class, and add or edit student records.

---

## Scheme Book — viewing and approving

Teachers submit scheme books (weekly teaching plans). Approval works in two steps:

1. **HOD approves** — The Head of Department approves first.
2. **You or the Deputy approve** — After HOD approval, you or the Deputy give final approval.

**To view all teachers’ scheme books:**

1. Go to **Academic** → **Scheme Book**.
2. Use **All teachers** to see everyone, or pick one teacher.
3. Use the **Class**, **Subject**, and **Term** filters as needed.

**To give final approval:**

- When a scheme shows **HOD approved: Yes** and **Final approved: —**, click the green tick (**Final approve**).

---

## Staff and user accounts

Go to **Administration** → **Staff** to manage teachers and other staff.

**Creating a new user account:**

1. Open the **Teachers** tab.
2. Click **Create User Account**.
3. Enter full name, email, temporary password, and phone (optional).
4. Under **Roles**, select one or more roles (e.g. **Teacher** and **Class Teacher** for someone who does both).
5. Click **Create Account**.

**Changing a user’s roles:**

1. In **Staff**, open the **Teachers** or **Non-Teaching Staff** tab.
2. Find the person and click **Manage roles**.
3. Tick or untick roles, then click **Save roles**.

**Adding a teacher (employment details):**

- After creating their user account, click **Add Teacher**, choose their profile, and fill in employee number, department, employment type, and other details.

## Quick reference

| Task | Where to go |
|------|------------------|
| Add a class | Academic → Academics → Classes → Add Class |
| Add a subject | Academic → Academics → Subjects → Add Subject |
| Approve scheme books | Academic → Scheme Book → Final approve (green tick) |
| Create staff account | Administration → Staff → Create User Account |
| Change staff roles | Administration → Staff → Manage roles |
| View term reports | Reports → Term Reports |
| View class performance | Reports → Class Analytics |

If something doesn’t work as expected, contact your system administrator.
